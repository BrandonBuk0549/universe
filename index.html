Different Galaxies Outside The MilkWay

The galaxy is interesting to learn about. There are two trilion galaxies outside of our planet Earth.

Irregular Galaxy

This galaxy is called Irregular galaxy because they have no central bulge or spiral.They are also the youngest in the galaxy and they are more likely collide with bigger galaxies due to thier small size.

Elpitical Galaxy

This is called Epliptical Galaxy because all Elliptical Galaxies have a supermassive black hole at their center. The size of the black hole is directly related to the size of the galaxy itself. They are generally made up of a lot of older stars that have a relatively low mass.

Whirlpool Galaxy

This galaxy was formed about 400 million years ago and is approximately 31 million light-years away from Earth. There are 100 billion stars in the whirlpool galaxy. This galaxy got its name from its distinct spiral structure.
